What is Coast FIRE?

Understanding the path to financial independence

Coast FIRE Explained

Coast FIRE is achieved when you've saved enough money that it will grow (without additional contributions) to support your retirement at traditional retirement age. Once you reach Coast FIRE, you can "coast" - meaning you no longer need to save for retirement, just cover your current expenses.

Key Benefits

  • Work flexibility and career changes
  • Reduced financial stress
  • Focus on lifestyle over saving rate
  • Earlier "financial freedom" milestone

Formula: Coast FIRE Number = FIRE Number รท (1 + Real Return Rate)^Years to Retirement
